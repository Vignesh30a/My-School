My School

A Small Introduction.

My school is DAV Boys Senior Secondary School, Mogappair. This is the school's website. The school teaches all grades from PreKG level to 12th grade. It has 5 sections per grade and approximately 40 students per section.

Syllabus

The school teaches CBSE portions with NCERT textbooks. It has 7 periods for grades 1-5, 8 periods for grades 6-8, and 9 periods for grades 9-12. It also has a class known as "Value Education" in which we are taught about important vedic values. We have some free time for finishing work. You can find the syllabus and textbooks in the website given above.
